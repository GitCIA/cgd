Garden Design

A thoughtful, design-led approach that balances aesthetics, function, and long-term sustainability — tailored to your space and lifestyle.

What’s included

  • Site assessment and constraints review (sun, soil, drainage, access)
  • Concept layouts and visual direction
  • Planting strategy, seasonal structure, and plant specifications
  • Materials and hardscape recommendations
  • Detailed plans suitable for construction and costing
